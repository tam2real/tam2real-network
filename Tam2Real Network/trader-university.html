[ BEFORE YOU TRADE REAL MONEY ]

Practice on demo until you fully understand support and resistance. Not just the concept — until you can spot them cold, under pressure, in real time. That's the standard. Don't move to real capital until you're there.

Demo trading isn't fake. It's rep work. Every hour on a paper account is building the pattern recognition that keeps you from blowing a real account in week one.

Be careful who you learn from. The right teacher changes lives. The wrong one destroys accounts and shakes confidence for years. If they're selling the lifestyle before they're teaching the craft, walk away. Study the charts, not the personality.
